Policy and Services Committee Recommends City Council Approve Retention of a

8. 
Direction on Strategies Related to Services for Unhoused Palo Alto Residents, Including 
Potential Emergency Shelter, and Adoption of a Resolution Endorsing the Santa Clara 
County Community Plan to End Homelessness 2020-25 (Continued from August 9, 
2021). 
MOTION:  Council Member Filseth moved, seconded by Council Member Cormack to:

Adopt Resolution 9984 endorsing the Santa Clara County “Community Plan to 
End Homelessness 2020-25”; and 
B.

team to the unhoused, which will include case management resources, additional 
Police resources, and program coordination staff.
