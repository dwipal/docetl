6. Review and Adoption of the Council Procedures and Protocols Handbook – Including Changes

MOTION: Council Member Veenker moved, seconded by Council Member Lauing to adopt the

including Procedures sections 2.3b(2)C, 3.1, 3.4 and 3.9(b(9) and Protocols Section 1.6d.

in advance the time when public comment cards will no longer be accepted.  
Section 5.1c1: strike “typically will” and replace with “may”
